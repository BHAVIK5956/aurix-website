Meet the Creator

The mind behind Aurix — a student, a builder, and a dreamer.

🧑‍💻

Hey, I'm Bhavik 👋

I'm a student from Bengaluru with an insatiable curiosity for AI engineering. I don't just study technology — I build it, break it, and rebuild it better.

What started as a personal AI companion project (Aurix) has grown into a full product suite. I believe AI shouldn't be locked behind expensive APIs and corporate walls. It should be personal, accessible, and something you can truly call your own.

My vision? To build AI products that genuinely help people — starting with students and creators who dream big but have limited resources. If I can build Aurix on a mid-range laptop with free tools, imagine what we can build together.

Currently preparing for KCET while building the future. Because why choose between studying and creating?
